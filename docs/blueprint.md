# **App Name**: Elos Platform

## Core Features:

- Fixed Navbar: Horizontal navbar with logo, menu items (Notícias, Biblioteca Digital, Comunidade, Suporte IA), and a login button.
- Hero Section: Hero section with a title, supporting text, and a placeholder for a welcoming image or illustration.
- Impactful Statistics Section: Statistics section highlighting the percentage and number of individuals with TEA and access to quality treatment.
- "O que você encontra aqui?" Section: Section showcasing the platform's offerings: Aprendizado, Conexão, and Organização, each with an icon, title, and description.
- "Você não está sozinho(a)!" Section: Community section with encouraging text, community cards (Cuidando com leveza, Dicas & Rotinas, Desabafos sem julgamentos), each with an icon, title, and description.
- "Profissionais capacitados avaliados" Section: Section showcasing professionals with circular photos, names, and a 'Ver perfil' link/button.
- "Acervo digital" Section: A section displaying a digital collection of resources with placeholders for document, video, or game thumbnails.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light purple (#A892EA), providing a sense of calm and care.
- Background color: Very light purple (#F1EFFB), almost white, creating a soft and clean base.
- Accent color: Soft blue (#92B7EA) complements the primary purple and evokes trust.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif combining modernity with warmth.
- Minimalist icons to represent different categories and actions. Micro-interactions on hover for buttons and cards.
- Generous spacing, cards with rounded corners, and soft shadows to provide a comfortable and accessible user experience.
- Subtle hover effects and transitions to enhance user interaction.
